Margarita

Alcoholic Cocktail glass

To make a classic Margarita drink, you'll need a few simple ingredients and some basic bartending skills. Start by gathering 2 oz of high-quality silver tequila, 1 oz of triple sec or orange liqueur, and 1 oz of freshly squeezed lime juice. Next, rim a margarita glass with salt by moistening the rim with a lime wedge and dipping it into a plate of salt. Fill a shaker with ice and add the tequila, triple sec, and lime juice. Shake vigorously for about 15-20 seconds to chill and mix the ingredients.

Ingredients
  • Tequila
  • Triple sec
  • Lime juice
  • salt
